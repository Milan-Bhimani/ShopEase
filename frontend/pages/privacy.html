Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

At ShopEase, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address
  • Payment Information: Card details (processed securely via payment gateway)
  • Order Information: Purchase history, delivery addresses
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, search queries, preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order updates and delivery notifications
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our products and services
  • Send promotional communications (with your consent)
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Delivery Partners: To fulfill and deliver your orders
  • Payment Processors: To process payments securely
  • Service Providers: For analytics and customer support
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted using 256-bit SSL encryption
  • Payment information is processed through PCI-DSS compliant systems
  • We implement strict access controls
  • Regular security audits are conducted

5. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Deletion: Request account deletion
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

6. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your login session
  • Store your cart items
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Personalize your experience

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at:
