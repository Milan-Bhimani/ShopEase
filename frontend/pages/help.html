Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Click on the "Login" button in the top right corner, then select "Create an account". Fill in your details including name, email, and password to get started.

Log in to your account and go to "My Orders" section. Click on any order to see its current status and tracking information. You'll also receive email and SMS updates.

We offer a 7-day easy return policy for most products. Items should be unused and in original packaging. Visit our Return Policy page for complete details.

Go to "My Orders" in your profile, find the order you want to cancel, and click "Cancel". Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped. Once shipped, you'll need to return the item.

We accept Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, Digital Wallets (Paytm, Amazon Pay), and Cash on Delivery. Visit our Payments page for more details.

Standard delivery takes 2-8 business days depending on your location. Metro cities typically receive orders faster. Check our Shipping page for detailed timelines.

If you've forgotten your password, log in to your account and go to the profile section. You can change your password there by entering your current password and a new one.
